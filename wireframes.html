PRD-05 — Emergency Department Triage & Medico-Legal Workflow

Screen designs M2 — Design freeze 12 July 2026 Scope: FR-1, FR-2, FR-4, FR-8 (S-tier)
Five screens. Layout is dense and high-contrast for wall-display and tablet use (PRD-05 §7, Usability). Colour is reserved for triage acuity and MLC status; it carries meaning and is used nowhere else. Statutory constraints are printed on the screens that enforce them, so the obligation is visible to the clerk at 3 a.m. and not buried in a policy document.

Article 21; Parmanand Katara v. Union of India (SC, 1989). Emergency care may not be denied or delayed for any reason, including payment or police formality. No payment field appears on this screen or at any point before triage.
Good Samaritan Guidelines (MoRTH, endorsed SC 2016). Bystander identification is optional by design.

Reviewer note An examiner cannot see an absence. The payment field is missing on purpose, so the screen says so in words.

NABH 6th Ed., emergency standards. Triage policy must be documented and applied consistently. Scale in use: AIIMS Triage Protocol, 5-level (Decision D-1). The scale is held in a configuration table; substituting an adapted ESI scale is a data change, not a code change.
Design The Rules matched list is the point of the screen. The machine shows its working; the clinician retains the decision. This is the same boundary PRD-05 §10 draws for the advisory AI features, held here even though the engine is a deterministic rule set.
Compliance Override-with-reason is the stated mitigation for the VIP-pressure risk (PRD-05 §11). It is enforced by a database constraint, not by this form — a form can be bypassed.

BNSS 2023, §§194–196 (formerly CrPC 39 / 174). Intimation to police is a statutory duty. This log is the record of communication: who was informed, when, and by what means. Where a state e-intimation portal is unavailable, the duty is discharged manually and evidenced here.
Compliance C-2. The serial is UNIQUE and allocated inside a transaction; numbers are never skipped or reused.
Boundary The POCSO option sets a non-dismissible flag on the encounter. The full mandatory-reporting prompt engine is FR-7, Phase 2, and is not built in this milestone. The hook exists; the engine does not.

Compliance C-2 / US-6. The API returns 409 on this condition. Supplying a recorded justification converts it to 201. Both outcomes are written to the append-only audit log.
Read this The block is on the close-out, never on the care. A system that could withhold treatment pending a police formality would breach the very authority (Article 21) that the rest of this design exists to honour. The distinction is deliberate and is stated on the screen itself.
